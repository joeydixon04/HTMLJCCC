ABOUT MAC MILLER

Mac Miller, born Malcolm James McCormick, was an American rapper, singer, and record producer. Known for his laid-back flow, introspective lyrics, and genre-spanning sound, Mac grew from Pittsburgh's underground scene into a respected artist. From mixtapes like "K.I.D.S." to albums like "Swimming" and "Circles," his work continues to influence modern hip-hop.

He tragically passed away in 2018, but his legacy lives on through his music and the impact he had on fans around the world.
